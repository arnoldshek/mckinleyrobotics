E Komo Mai!

We are a FIRST robotics team from President William McKinley High School in beautiful Honolulu, Hawaii. Our team was established in 1999. We offer students endless opportunities to enrich their education through practical applications of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math. We are looking forward to the 2018 FRC Power Up season.
